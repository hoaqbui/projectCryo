## Combat Tunables
*   **Moves**: All units have 2 moves (fixed).
*   **Actions**: All units have 2 actions (fixed).
*   **Luck Impact**: Units with high Luck (LUK > 50) have a 25% higher chance to trigger secondary effects (stuns, burns, double energy).
